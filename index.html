I work on AI optimization and GPU acceleration at AMD, focusing on deep learning workloads, domain adaptation, and high-performance computing challenges. With a Ph.D. in Computer Vision and 15+ publications in conferences like IEEE WACV and journals like Computers in Biology and Medicine, I'm passionate about bridging the gap between research and practice.
